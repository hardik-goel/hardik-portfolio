Hey fellow writers 👋

I recently published my book, Forgotten by Design: The Silent Epidemic of Digital Dementia, and I’ve realized something powerful — readers don’t just want a book, they want a discovery journey.

So here’s a simple idea 💡

Instead of spending on ads or begging algorithms for reach...
let’s help each other organically.

🔁 The “Share-for-Share” Pact

You share my book with your list, and I’ll share yours with mine.
No strings. No payments.
Just mutual exposure to fresh, curious readers.

If your audience loves tech, psychology, AI, or modern society — they’ll connect with my work.
And I’m happy to feature your book too — as long as it aligns with thought-provoking, human-centered themes.

📬 Here’s How It Works

  1. Drop your book link + short blurb (2 lines max) in the comments or reply to this email.

  2. I’ll feature 3–5 books from this thread in my next Substack issue.

  3. You feature Forgotten by Design in your next issue or newsletter.

Simple. Fair. Zero cost. Maximum value.

🧠 About My Book

Forgotten by Design: The Silent Epidemic of Digital Dementia
A deep dive into how our dependence on devices is quietly erasing our ability to think, remember, and connect.
It’s not anti-tech — it’s pro-awareness.

Let’s prove collaboration > competition.
